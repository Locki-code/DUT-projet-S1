Internships

The internship is an important part of the student's training.
This first contact with the reality of the profession should enable him to synthesize the knowledge acquired at the IUT, to become aware of the socio-professional environment and to specify his personal aptitudes.
The subject of the internship must be identified by the company and validated by the department after consultation.
During the course of the internship, the department provides a follow-up internship requiring exchange of information between the company and the department. This monitoring should be carried out, as far as possible, by visits from the teachers to the place of the course.
At the end of his / her internship, the student must support an internship report before a jury including the internship supervisor in the company, the teacher tutor of the trainee and another permanent teacher of the department.
This report and this defense are formal and each gives rise to a qualitative and quantitative evaluation.


Internship evaluation

The internship is evaluated jointly by the company / organization (company / organization tutor) and the department (tutor and jury) on the following elements:

  • Work in a company / organization, with regard to the objectives set in the agreement
  • The written report, framed in its form, highlighting the skills implemented during the internship
  • Oral defense by a mixed jury company / organization, department

For these three elements, the assessment of the trainee must relate to:


  • His ability to use his academic achievements in carrying out his mission
  • Learning outcomes resulting from immersion in the workplace: technical skills and relational skills in reference to the business and skills benchmark of the DUT informatique
