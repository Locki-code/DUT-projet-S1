Going abroad

If some students wish to complete a semester studying abroad during their second year of DUT or during their year of professional license they can.
To do so ,the students must apply during the year before their departure.
Moreover, they will be able to complete their second year or professional license internship as a graduation project in a university laboratory of a partner institution.

Come study at IUT

All courses at Lens' IUT are taught in French. A good understanding level of of French language is consequently required.
To get DUT and professional licenses, B2 level is required.
It corresponds to the target level needed for baccalaureate exam.
