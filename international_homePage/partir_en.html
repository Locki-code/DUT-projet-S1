bilateral convention

Conférence des Recteurs Et Principaux des Universités du Québec - (Quebec)

CREPUQ (abridged version) group all the universities from Quebec, mainly composed of french speakers, and even a university of english speakers.

If a student wishes to achieve a semester in one of the universty from the CREPUQ-BCI's program must leave an application file depending on the appropriate procedure to the International Relationships of Artois university throughout the year predating the departure.

If the IUT allow you to go, your application file will be delivered to the host' university, wich have the right to decline or accept your proposal.

  • Who's concerned : every second year students from every section of IUT – semester 4
  • How much students : unlimited
  • Class' langage : french or english
  • Mobility periods : from the begining of January to the end of April

ABROAD INTERNSHIP

Students can effectuate their 2nd year internship or their License degree in the form as a end-of-the-year-project a laboratory located in an IUT'S partner institute. Dans ce cadre :

  • Pupils currently on their first year of DUT apply on June for their 2nd year.
  • Students from License degree apply in September for their end-of-the-year internship.

Students also have the possibility to find an internship abroad by their own.

Informations meetings would be made regurarly to allow the pupils staying up to date of wich step they'll have to do, this can vary in an another country.

The personal from the Lens' IUT help and work along with the attendant since he's accepted untill they're installed over there.

LANGAGES STANDARD

Depending on the land, langages standard can variate. The references indicated for each destination corresponds to the Cadre Européen de Référence pour les langues criterion.

Langages teachers from every department / sector from the IUT will therefore test the langages skills from the student.

The Langages House of Artois Universities give you the opportunity to reinforce your langage level and thereby register you for a free test. And this for every students.

FINANCIAL HELP

There's many fincancial help for the futures graduates to realise their mobilities either for study or do an on-the-job-training. The eligibility conditions and the amount of money requiered may vary.

More information about financing and requesting terms to the International Relationships Service of Artois University.

Some advices for an sojourn abroad

  • Establish your budget
  • Organize the transfer from the airport and how to get there
  • Copy important documents such as your identification papers
  • Learn a few words in the local language or English to communicate with ease and live better on site.
  • Obtain the necessary prescriptions if there are drug treatments that need to be followed daily or regularly.
  • Note important phone numbers
  • Determine your mobile phone package
