Study possibilities in France

All the courses at the IUT in Lens are taught in French. A good level of understanding of the French language is therefore essential. For DUT and professional licenses, level B2 is required. This corresponds to the target level for the baccalaureate test. The Ministry of National Education and Higher Education explains this level of competence as follows: « "Can understand the essentials of a concrete or abstract subject in a complex text, including a technical discussion in his speciality. He can communicate with a degree of spontaneity and fluency, such as a conversation with a native speaker. The student can give an opinion on a topical subject and discuss it "

 

The IUT offers diplomas in 4 fields accessible to foreign students either to come to study in training on an individual basis, or as part of an agreement signed with a partner institution of IUT for targeted training.

Trade sector

  • DUT Marketing techniques (TC)
  • LP Marketing of Products and Services – Marketing of Inter-company Goods and Services

Management sector

  • DUT Management of Companies and Administrations (GEA)

Informatique sector

  • DUT Informatique

Digital media sector

  • DUT Digital Media (MMI)
  • LP Digital professions: web design, writing and production (TAIS) –web design, writing and production (TAIS)
